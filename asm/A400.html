A400: Item table
Used by the routines at 87CA and 93D1.
The location of item N (0xAD<=N<=0xFF) is defined by the pair of bytes at addresses A400+N and A500+N. The meaning of the bits in each byte-pair is as follows:
Bit(s) Meaning
15 Most significant bit of the y-coordinate
14 Collection flag (reset=collected, set=uncollected)
8-13 Room number
5-7 Least significant bits of the y-coordinate
0-4 x-coordinate
